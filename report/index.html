UN Sustainable Development Goal

The objective of goal 12 is to ensure sustainable consumption of our resources. We are currently using more natural resources than which are available. Our population keeps growing and in turn our consumption increases. Our current planet is not able to sustain the ever growing demand that we put on it.

About 1/3 of all food that is produced gets wasted. We wanted to focus on these issues and take a look at the food we consume and what kind of effects it has on the planet. We realised that a lot of food gets wasted, food that in many cases are perfectly edible but are thrown away because of our picky habits.

Food Waste Issue

“In agriculture in Sweden, about 300,000 tonnes of raw material is produced per year, which was intended for food but which does not go on to food. One of the reasons for this is the market's quality requirements, in the later stages of the chain and among us consumers. “
the Swedish Board of Agriculture

“The production of the amount of food thrown every year corresponds to emissions of about 2 million tons of carbon dioxide, which is about 3 percent of the total amount of greenhouse gas emissions in Sweden.”
Swedish Environmental Protection Agency

Ideating

Through the ideation session we worked with the key topics and issues we had identified through our research. These issues were food waste and production shortcomings where we sketched and thought through possible ways to make these topics into speculative design. From the ideation we had a couple ideas that we decided to work further on.

Some of these ideas were VR glasses that would guide you through the store and change the way products look, giving you a false perspective of what you are buying. Another was a way to alter the way your food looks, similarly using ”ugly” food and making it look fresh and nice. From the critique session we got valuable feedback and we were able to narrow it down to an app that could alter the way your fruit looks from unfresh to very fresh, in order to harvest "Likes" on social media.
